History

The NBA would not be what it is today without those that came before. This page is dedicated to those of NBA past! Discussions here can range from the original rules of the game at the formation of the NBA, to recently retired veterans and their contributions to the league, and everything in between.

For anyone interested in learning/seeing more from NBA's past, head to NBA History page on Instagram! There's plenty to see there, from more recent history all the way back to the beginnings of the league, with a little bit of everything in-between!
