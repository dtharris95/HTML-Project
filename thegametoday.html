The Game Today

The NBA is larger and more global than ever before! It is on the backs of those that came before that the league stands on today. Because of their contributions to the league, the NBA is one of the most popular leagues around the world! This is evident in the conversations had surrounding the league on Twitter, in an unofficial community known to fans as "NBA Twitter", not to be confused with the NBA's official Twitter page. Since NBA Twitter has no official page, you can find discussions amongst fans via going to Twitter's search bar and entering any NBA related news.

Basketball Reference is a fantastic website for finding information on active players in the league, current rosters, stats on the season, and more! Join in on the discussion below.
